The Will B Foundation Grand Raffle 2016 Official Rules & Regulations

  1. Only the first 600 paid tickets received by the raffle committee will be registered into the drawing. Winner need not be present.
  2. Ticket price is $ 100.00 per ticket. Raffle tickets may be purchased before and up until approximately 8 PM the night of the drawing.
  3. Net proceeds will benefit the Will B Foundation. The Will B Foundation is a 501(c)(3) Organization.
  4. The Grand Raffle will take place on October 6, 2016 at approximately 8:30 PM at Belvedere Banquets located at 1170 W Devon Ave in Elk Grove Village.
  5. Acceptance of a prize constitutes permission for The Will B Foundation to use the name, photograph, testimonial and/or other likeness the prize information for promotional, advertising and/or publicity in any media without compensation, notice or further consent to the extent permitted by law.
  6. Checks that are denied or returned for insufficient funds or for other reasons will be deemed void. The corresponding ticket will be voided and not entered into the raffle.
  7. The primary person/s as named on the ticket will be awarded the prize and will be responsible for all taxes (Federal, State and Local) incurred. No portion of the cost of the ticket is tax deductible as a charitable contribution, but can be used to offset gains associated with raffle, bingo, or other chance game winnings. The winner shall provide the Will B Foundation with their social security number and/or EIN.
  8. The Will B Foundation is required by law to issue 1099 as designated by the Internal Revenue Services on winnings of over $600. Prize money will be released to the winner/s upon receipt of the required 1099 tax information.
  9. Board members and volunteers of The Will B Foundation are eligible to win the drawing.
  10. By entering, entrants accept and agree to the official rules and the decisions of The Will B Foundation which are final and binding in all respects.
