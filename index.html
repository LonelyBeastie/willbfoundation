Who Is Will B?

Will Belomonte fights a disease called Cystic Fibrosis, also known as CF. CF causes severe damage to the lungs and digestive system. Will's cells produce thick sticky mucus that traps bacteria making him susceptible to lung infections, sinus infections, and digestive issues. None of this stops Will from his mission to make a difference so there WILL B a cure for Cystic Fibrosis.
